Omelette

Description

An omelette is the perfect breakfast dish. In contrast to things like oats or cereal, a good omelette will keep you full untill lunch.

Also, an omelette is incredibly easy and fast to make. It can be made in 10 minutes give or take.

Ingredients

  • 5 Eggs
  • Cheese of choice
  • Meat of choice
  • Some vegetables(optional)

Steps

  1. Turn stove on to medium heat
  2. Crack eggs into bowl, add salt and whisk together
  3. Pour egg mass onto pan. Everytime the edges of the egg mass sets, scoop it into the middle.
  4. Once cooked, pour in cheese, meat and vegetables, and then fold the omelette
  5. Enjoy your delicious omelette
